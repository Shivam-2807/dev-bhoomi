WELCOME TO DBGI SAHARANPUR

Dev Bhoomi Group of Institutions (DBGI), Saharanpur campus, established in 2009, is a premier group of institutions under Uttarakhand Uthan Samiti, a non-profit society professionally managed by eminent academicians, industrialists, and scientists.

Keeping pace with rapid technological advancements, the Samiti remains committed to providing quality education and has established six temples of learning and innovation.

DBGI is one of the fastest-growing engineering colleges in the Saharanpur district. The campus is located approximately 4 km from Civil Hospital towards the D.M. Residence at Beri Jama, Saharanpur.

The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport, situated about 95 km from the institute.

The sprawling main campus is spread across 150 bighas (approximately 22 acres) of lush greenery, surrounded by rich flora, fauna, and the National Forest.
